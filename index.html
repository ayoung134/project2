♥ RESERVE YOUR SEAT IN THE QUEEN'S COURT ♥



Throughout the year, The Queen of Hearts welcomes guests to step into an unforgettable, one of a kind experience.

Inspired by the fever-dream elegance of Victorian opulence and underground prohibition decadence, our bar blends deep crimson hues, gilded mirrors, flickering chandeliers, and the quiet hum of secrets being traded over rare spirits.





Trial By Fire Fridays

Have an unforgettable night with our signature flaming cocktails, dazzling performances, vibrant music, and spectacular bursts of firelight!

Velvet Rebellion Saturdays

Step into a night of rhythm and revelry with electro-swing DJs, grand midnight proclamations, and cascading champagne towers

Tarot and Tonic Sundays

Wander through a night of whispered tarot fortunes, shimmering botanical elixirs, flickering candlelit corners, intimate confessions, enchanted readings, and sparkling drinks that ignite your imagination.
